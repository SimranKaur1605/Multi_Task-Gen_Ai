**Summary:**

A DC motor is an electrical machine that converts direct current into mechanical energy through magnetic field interactions. The main components include the armature, commutator, brushes, field winding, and rotor. **Key points:**

* DC motors are widely used in applications such as electric vehicles, robotics, and industrial machinery due to their simple speed control and reliable performance.
* They provide high starting torque and smooth operation, making them suitable for various mechanical systems.
* The motor's operation is based on the production of a magnetic force when electric current flows through the armature winding, causing the rotor to rotate.

**Important conclusion:** DC motors are a reliable and efficient choice for a range of applications, from small to large-scale mechanical systems, due to their simple design and high performance characteristics.
